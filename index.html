Some backstory

This is a game about a unicorn called Charlie.
One day when Charlie was out for a stroll in the woods he
noticed something strange in the sky.
Everything went quiet.
Dark clouds started forming in the sky and he could hear thunder rumbling.
The bright sunny forest had now become dark and gloomy.
Even the trees felt different, hauntingly. Where did all the animals go?
Charlie saw no signs of them...
He got an eerie feeling
It felt almost like he was beeing watched...
